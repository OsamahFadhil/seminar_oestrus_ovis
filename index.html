01 / Introduction

What is Oestrus ovis?

Nasal myiasis is a parasitic disease of sheep caused by the larvae of Oestrus ovis. The adult female deposits live larvae directly into the nostrils, where they develop within the nasal passages and sinuses, leading to irritation and inflammation of the upper respiratory tract.

Infection is especially prevalent during warm seasons â€” seasonal awareness is a critical part of prevention and early diagnosis.
